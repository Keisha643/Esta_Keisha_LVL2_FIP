Our Story

Swerve is a Canadian beverage company established in 1960, originally known for its bold alcoholic beverages and cocktail mixes. In 2016, Swerve expanded into the athletic market with a line of performance‑focused sports drinks. Today, Swerve stands at the intersection of energy, flavour, and lifestyle, offering products for both celebration and performance.
